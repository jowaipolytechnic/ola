FAQ

What should I do with this form?

  • Read the eligibility criteria
  • Fill all the required fields.
  • Submit the Application on or before the last date.
  • Note the Counselling Day as given in the Portal.
  • Download the Application Form and print.
  • On the day of counselling carry the Printed Form, 2 photos, all original documents and their photocopies
  • Hand over the above to the Counsellors in the Polytechnic.
  • Login to the portal from time to time to receive messages.

Should I fill every field in the Form?

Yes, as far as practicable.

Fields in green rounded rectangle are mandatory field. You should fill these fields otherwise the system will not allow you to submit the Form.

I don't have a mobile. What should I do?

A mobile number is a mandatory requirement.

We will contact you on this number for numerous purpose. Use your Parent/Relatives number for the purpose.

Make sure you obtain their permission beforehand.

What is the difference between the Save and Submit button?

Use the Save button when you have to break your work. Later you can login again and continue from where you left.

Remember you can edit all data when you use the Save button.

The Submit button on the other hand should be used when you have entered all the data.

Once you press the Submit button, you will not be able to edit again.

What if I realised I had entered wrong data after submitting the Application?

Report the matter to the Counsellors during your Counselling Session.

Do not make changes on the downloaded form by yourself as this may lead to disqualification of your candidature.

I cannot save or submit my Application.

This problem occur either because of Internet Connection problem or heavy server traffic.

Avoid filling your Form on the last minute to avoid heavy server traffic.

About document upload.

The system accepts only image file with the extension of .png/.jpg/.jpeg and also pdf file.

You can use your phone to take pictures of your document or you can scan them.

IMPORTANT: Make sure they are readable.

It is not advisable to upload big file because they will take a long time and possibly break the upload process.Files as low as 50Kb are good enough

In the unlikely event that your document contains more than one page, you can upload it as a multipage pdf document.

You can use a scanner or a scanner app of mobile phone to make pdf files.

Downloading the Application Form

After you completed all steps, a message from Jowai Polytechnic Online Application will appear.

For PC

Click on the "Open and Download My Application Form" link.

The Application Form will open in a new tab on your browser.

You can print the Form directly from here.

For Android phones

Hold for one to two seconds on the "Open and Download My Application Form" link.

A menu will appear, touch on "Open in new tab" option.

The Form will appear in a new window.

Open the new tab.

Touch the 3-dot menu at the top right corner, choose "Share", then "Print" and then "Save as PDF".

Touch the PDF icon and choose the folder where you want to store the file.
